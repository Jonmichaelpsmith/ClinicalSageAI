Key Features

Our Vault™ Workspace offers industry-leading document management capabilities with advanced AI integration.

Auto-Retention Scheduler

Intelligent document lifecycle management with configurable policies, automatic archiving, and notification capabilities.

  • Configurable retention periods based on document types
  • Automatic document archiving before deletion
  • Email notifications for approaching retention limits
  • Comprehensive audit trails with SHA-256 integrity hashing
  • Manual override through CLI tools

Role-Based Dashboards

Personalized workspace experience tailored to each user's role in the organization with relevant tasks and documents.

  • Role-specific document views and workflows
  • Personalized task lists and priorities
  • Customizable widgets and metrics
  • Seamless switching between roles for users with multiple responsibilities
  • Real-time notifications center

SOP Training Workflow

Automates the assignment and tracking of SOP training tasks when documents reach "Effective" status.

  • Automatic assignment of training tasks based on user roles
  • Configurable training deadlines and escalation paths
  • Knowledge verification through quizzes and assessments
  • Training compliance dashboards for managers
  • 21 CFR Part 11 compliant training records

Site-Startup Checklist Builder

Creates comprehensive site activation checklists tailored to country-specific regulatory requirements.

  • AI-generated checklists based on region and study type
  • Automatic tracking of document submission status
  • Real-time completion metrics and progress tracking
  • Site-specific customization capabilities
  • Regulatory intelligence updates for changing requirements

Promotional Review (MLR) Workflow

Streamlines the medical, legal, and regulatory review process for promotional materials with AI analysis.

  • AI-powered claim analysis to identify unsupported statements
  • Reference linking between promotional content and supporting evidence
  • Sequential or parallel review workflows
  • Comment aggregation and resolution tracking
  • Version comparison with redlining capabilities

Quality Events Tracking

Comprehensive system for managing quality events, deviations, and CAPA processes in a compliant framework.

  • Structured event reporting and categorization
  • Root cause analysis with AI-suggested potential causes
  • CAPA management with due date tracking
  • Effectiveness checks and verification
  • Trend analysis and quality metrics dashboards

Enterprise-Grade Security

TrialSage Vault™ implements the highest security standards to protect your sensitive regulatory data.

Multi-Layered Security Architecture

Our security approach includes multiple defense layers to provide comprehensive protection for your sensitive documents.

  • Zero Trust Architecture: Every access request is fully authenticated, authorized, and encrypted
  • SHA-256 Integrity Hashing: Tamper-evident audit trails for all document operations
  • Multi-tenant Isolation: Complete data segregation between organizational tenants
  • Role-Based Access Control: Fine-grained permissions for precise access management
  • Blockchain Verification: Immutable record of document transitions and approvals
  • Advanced Encryption: Data encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • API Security: Rate limiting, validation, and Helmet-protected endpoints
