AI-Powered Modules

Each TrialSage™ module is enhanced with specialized AI capabilities tailored to specific regulatory documentation challenges

IND Wizard™ AI

The IND Wizard™ module incorporates advanced document intelligence to streamline IND preparation and submission. It automatically identifies required documentation based on product type and therapeutic area, and validates content against FDA expectations.

  • Intelligent Gap Analysis: AI scans existing documentation to identify missing elements needed for IND submission, prioritizing them by importance.
  • Automated Content Generation: Generates draft content for standard IND sections with factual accuracy guardrails and citations for all sources.
  • Pre-submission Validation: Checks for regulatory inconsistencies, formatting issues, and missing references before FDA submission.

CSR Intelligence™ AI

CSR Intelligence™ employs advanced natural language processing to extract meaningful insights from clinical study reports, enabling pattern recognition across studies and accelerating the review process.

  • Semantic Search: Natural language querying across all CSR content without requiring exact keyword matches.
  • Adverse Event Pattern Detection: Automatically identifies and flags potential safety signals across multiple studies.
  • Consistency Verification: Ensures alignment between protocols, statistical analysis plans, and final reports.

Study Architect™ AI

Study Architect™ provides AI-powered protocol design assistance, helping clinical teams create more efficient and scientifically rigorous study designs while preventing common protocol issues.

  • Powering Optimization: AI-based sample size calculations and simulations based on historical trial data.
  • Protocol Error Prevention: Identifies potential protocol design flaws, ambiguities, and operational challenges.
  • Scientific Literature Integration: Automatically suggests relevant citations and scientific backing for protocol elements.

TrialSage Vault™ AI

TrialSage Vault™ leverages document intelligence to transform static documents into searchable knowledge, automatically organizing and categorizing content while maintaining regulatory compliance.

  • Intelligent Document Categorization: Automatically classifies uploaded documents based on content analysis.
  • Contextual Search: Understanding of regulatory terminology enables searching by concept rather than just keywords.
  • Automated Metadata Extraction: Identifies key information (study IDs, dates, product names) without manual tagging.
