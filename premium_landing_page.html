Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about TrialSage™ and our regulatory intelligence platform.

How does TrialSage™ ensure regulatory compliance?

TrialSage™ maintains a comprehensive database of regulatory guidelines from major authorities (FDA, EMA, PMDA, NMPA) that is continuously updated. Our ICH Wiz™ module provides real-time compliance checks against these guidelines, ensuring your documents meet the latest requirements. Additionally, our system learns from successful submissions to provide insights on approval pathways.

Can TrialSage™ integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, TrialSage™ offers robust API capabilities that allow for seamless integration with your existing document management systems, CTMS, EDC platforms, and other regulatory tools. Our Enterprise plan includes dedicated integration support to ensure a smooth transition and interoperability with your current workflows.

How does the CSR Intelligence™ module work?

CSR Intelligence™ utilizes advanced natural language processing to analyze over 3,200 clinical study reports from public databases and anonymized client submissions. The system extracts key elements such as study design, endpoints, inclusion/exclusion criteria, and regulatory outcomes to provide insights for your submissions. You can search for precedents in your therapeutic area or query specific regulatory questions.

What security measures does TrialSage™ implement?

TrialSage™ employs enterprise-grade security including SOC 2 Type II compliance, HIPAA compliance, end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. All data is stored in secure, redundant data centers with 99.99% uptime. We also offer private cloud deployments for Enterprise customers with specific security requirements.

How long does it take to implement TrialSage™?

Implementation timelines vary based on your organization's size and specific requirements. Typically, Starter and Professional plans can be fully operational within 2-4 weeks, while Enterprise implementations may take 4-8 weeks depending on the extent of customization and integration needed. Our dedicated implementation team works closely with you to ensure a smooth onboarding process.

Can TrialSage™ handle submissions for multiple regions?

Yes, TrialSage™ is designed to support global regulatory submissions across major markets including FDA (US), EMA (Europe), PMDA (Japan), NMPA (China), Health Canada, and other regional authorities. Our platform automatically adapts document templates and formats to meet region-specific requirements while maintaining consistency in core content.

What training and support do you provide?

All plans include comprehensive onboarding training for users. Professional and Enterprise plans include role-based training sessions, admin training, and customized workflow configuration. Our support options range from email support for Starter plans to 24/7 dedicated support for Enterprise customers. We also provide a comprehensive knowledge base, regular webinars, and an active user community.

How does Study Architect™ optimize clinical trial design?

Study Architect™ leverages historical clinical trial data and regulatory precedents to suggest optimal protocol designs. The system uses statistical modeling to simulate different trial scenarios, helping you optimize sample size, endpoint selection, inclusion/exclusion criteria, and statistical analysis plans. This can significantly reduce protocol amendments and increase the likelihood of regulatory approval.
