AI-Powered Features

We've harnessed the power of artificial intelligence to transform regulatory workflows, creating a seamless experience from protocol design to submission.

Protocol Blueprint Generator

Create comprehensive protocol drafts in minutes instead of weeks. Simply enter your basic study parameters and watch as our AI crafts a detailed protocol outline.

  • Auto-generates 70% of protocol text, eliminating repetitive work
  • Creates complete study framework including rationale, objectives, and assessment schedules
  • Reduces protocol development time from weeks to hours

Adaptive Design Simulator

Test and validate your protocol design before implementation with powerful simulation tools that visualize outcomes and optimize your approach.

  • Visualize power curves and enrollment timelines in real-time
  • Predict the impact of dropout rates on your study outcomes
  • Test parallel, crossover, and adaptive arm designs without complex programming

Intelligent Endpoint Advisor

Select the right endpoints for your study based on industry standards and historical success patterns from thousands of previous trials.

  • Access an AI-curated library of validated endpoints from over 5,000 trials
  • Receive customized recommendations for your specific therapeutic area
  • Align with CDISC/SDTM standards automatically

CRF & SDTM Variable Mapper

Streamline your data management with automatic mapping between your case report forms and standardized datasets.

  • Create a complete CDISC-compliant data pipeline with one click
  • Automatically suggest appropriate CRF fields based on your protocol
  • Validate terminology against industry standards like LOINC and MedDRA

Risk-Based Monitoring Planner

Identify and mitigate risks before they impact your study with an AI-powered monitoring approach based on historical patterns.

  • Auto-detect high-risk data fields and sites based on historical deviation patterns
  • Generate comprehensive monitoring plans that meet ICH E6(R3) guidelines
  • Create ready-to-use CAPA templates for common issues

Global Compliance Checker

Ensure your protocol meets requirements across all target regions with automatic regional compliance checking.

  • Instantly identify sections needing regional customization
  • Receive suggested language for EU, PMDA, and other regional requirements
  • Create submission-ready documents for multiple jurisdictions simultaneously
