FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about TrialSage™ and how it can transform your regulatory process.

How does TrialSage™ use AI in regulatory document generation?

TrialSage™ uses advanced natural language processing and domain-specific AI models trained on thousands of regulatory documents to automate content generation. The AI understands regulatory context, document structure requirements, and can transform raw clinical data into well-structured narratives that follow ICH and regional authority guidelines.

The system can generate first drafts of protocols, clinical study reports, INDs, and other regulatory documents while maintaining scientific accuracy and regulatory compliance. All AI-generated content is fully transparent and remains under human review and control.

What regulatory regions and submission types does TrialSage™ support?

TrialSage™ supports the full range of global regulatory submissions including:

  • FDA (US): IND, NDA, BLA, 510(k), PMA, and annual reports
  • EMA (EU): Clinical Trial Applications, Marketing Authorization Applications
  • PMDA (Japan): J-NDA and clinical trial notifications
  • NMPA (China): Clinical trial applications and marketing applications

The platform is continuously updated to reflect the latest regulatory requirements in each region.

How does the CSR Intelligence™ module work?

CSR Intelligence™ provides access to our proprietary database of 3,217+ parsed clinical study reports across therapeutic areas. The system has extracted and structured key information including:

  • Study design parameters and endpoints
  • Patient population characteristics
  • Statistical methodologies
  • Safety and efficacy outcomes

Users can search by indication, phase, endpoint, population criteria, and other parameters to find relevant precedents for protocol design, endpoint selection, and statistical planning.

What security measures does TrialSage™ implement?

TrialSage™ implements comprehensive security measures to protect sensitive regulatory and clinical data:

  • SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified infrastructure
  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls with multi-factor authentication
  • Detailed audit trails for all document access and modifications
  • Regular penetration testing and security assessments
  • FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliant electronic signatures

Our platform is designed to meet the stringent security requirements of pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

How long does implementation take?

TrialSage™ can be implemented quickly with our structured onboarding process:

  • Cloud deployment: 1-2 weeks for core functionality
  • Enterprise integration: 4-8 weeks depending on existing systems
  • Custom configurations: 2-4 additional weeks for company-specific templates and workflows

Our implementation team works closely with your regulatory, clinical, and IT stakeholders to ensure a smooth transition and proper integration with your existing processes.
