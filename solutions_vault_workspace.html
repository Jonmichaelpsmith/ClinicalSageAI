2.7.1.1 Background and Overview

ENZYMAX FORTE (dantrolene sodium) Tablets are intended for the chronic treatment of spasticity of voluntary muscles. The current application is for a new strength (75 mg) of dantrolene sodium to complement the existing 25 mg and 50 mg strengths.
