Section 2.7.1 - Summary of Biopharmaceutic Studies and Analytical Methods

This section should provide a comprehensive summary of all biopharmaceutic studies conducted with the drug substance and drug product. The focus should be on critical studies that provide an understanding of how the drug substance and drug product are absorbed, distributed, metabolized, and excreted.

Key areas to address include:

  • Bioavailability (BA) studies evaluating the rate and extent of absorption
  • Comparative BA and bioequivalence (BE) studies
  • In vitro-in vivo correlation studies
  • Bioanalytical methods and validation
